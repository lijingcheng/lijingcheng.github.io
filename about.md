### 🙋 你好 / Hi

我是风行，iOS 独立开发者，base 北京。球场上热衷抢篮板，山野里是个独行者。相信好的产品值得反复打磨，好的风景值得走很远的路去看。如果有一天不上班了，就骑上小摩托去更远的地方。

I'm Feng Xing, an indie iOS developer based in Beijing. I crash the boards on the court and walk ridgelines solo in the mountains. Great products take iteration; great views take the long trail. One day I'll trade the desk for a motorcycle and ride somewhere far.

</div>

<div class="about-section">

### 🚀 在做什么 / Building

独立开发面向全球市场的移动应用，从产品设计到 UI/UE，再到代码，再到推广，一人全栈出海。

Solo indie dev shipping mobile apps to the global market — from design to code to launch.

</div>

<div class="about-section">

### 💡 理念 / Philosophy

对技术追求完美，但更在意屏幕另一边的人用起来是否顺手。如果你对我的 App 有建议，或只是想说声 hi，随时给我发邮件。

I chase clean code, but I care more about the person on the other side of the screen. Have a suggestion? Just want to say hi? Drop me an email anytime.
